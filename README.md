# 🌍 Renewable Energy Benchmarking Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard that benchmarks renewable energy performance of IsDB (Islamic Development Bank) Member Countries against global trends from **2018–2025**.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes renewable energy adoption across countries using Power BI. It compares IsDB Member Countries with global averages, highlighting trends in renewable electricity generation, renewable energy consumption, and country-level performance.

# 🎯 Objectives

- Compare renewable energy performance between IsDB and non-IsDB countries.
- Analyze renewable electricity generation trends from 2018–2025.
- Identify the top-performing countries based on renewable energy indicators.
- Monitor renewable energy growth using interactive KPIs and visualizations.
- Enable dynamic exploration through filters and slicers.

---


# 📌 Key Insights

- **Non-IsDB member countries consistently outperformed IsDB member countries** in renewable electricity share throughout the 2018–2025 period.
- The **highest average renewable electricity share** for non-IsDB countries was **48.59% in 2025**, compared to **33.90% in 2019**, representing a **43.36% increase**.
- IsDB member countries reached their highest average renewable share of **28.53% in 2024**, improving from **23.27% in 2018**.
- The performance gap between IsDB and non-IsDB countries was largest in **2025**, with non-IsDB countries averaging **24.97 percentage points** higher renewable electricity share.
- Total hydroelectricity generation was significantly higher among **non-IsDB countries (24,656.86 TWh)** than **IsDB member countries (635.56 TWh)**.
- **China** contributed approximately **41%** of total hydroelectricity generation among non-IsDB countries, highlighting its dominant role in global renewable energy production.
- The dashboard enables interactive comparison of renewable energy performance by **country, year, and IsDB membership**, supporting benchmarking and trend analysis.
